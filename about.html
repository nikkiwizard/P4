    Gallery Commission Me

 Nice to meet you! My name is Nicole Campos and I'm currently a senior at NJIT. A long time passion of mine has been knitting and crochet. My mom taught me how to crochet 10 years ago, and I've been hooked ever since. I started off simple, making scarves and simple baskets. A few years later, I taught myself how to knit thanks to YouTube videos. I refined my skills and now I can make just about anything. You can see in the Gallery page that I've made baby blankets, matching hats and scarves, amigurumi, and evem a sweater for my teddy bear.
 I made this page to share my love of knit and crochet with all of you! Maybe something you see will inspire you to make your own creations. Or, you may even want me to make something for you or a loved one. Please enjoy exploring my website.
